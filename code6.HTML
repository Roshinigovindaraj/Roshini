A HISTORIAN

historian is a person who studies and writes about the past and is regarded as an authority on it.
Historians are concerned with the continuous, methodical narrative and research of past events as relating to the human race;
as well as the study of all history in time

I have a profound passion for the history of the Tamil language and Tamil kings, especially the Chola dynasty.
My interest spans across the entire history of Tamil Nadu, delving into the rich heritage and influential rulers of the region.
The Cholas, renowned for their contributions to Tamil culture and architecture, are a particular focus of my studies. Additionally, I am deeply intrigued by the concept of Lemuria, also referred to as Kumari Kandam.
This legendary lost land is a significant subject of my research, reflecting my fascination with ancient Tamil civilization and its myths. I have explored numerous aspects of Lemuria, from its supposed geographical location to its cultural impact. This ongoing research into Tamil history and Lemuria is not just an academic pursuit but a true passion, driving me to uncover and understand the intricate details of Tamil heritage and its historical narratives.
